Welcome

A Place to Gather, Create & Grow

Our location offers a park-like setting with sweeping views and direct access to the Kankakee River. The River View Great Room houses many treasured touchstones from MKP's origins — including the MKP Altar, talking sticks, and Day 1 Year 1 artifacts.

We are just 5 minutes from thriving, artsy downtown Kankakee, Illinois — a friendly, accommodating, and welcoming community. Bring your group and enjoy a setting designed for meaningful experiences.

Your Time Here

The Journey Unfolds

From arrival to departure, every part of your stay is held with care and intention — woven into a story your group will carry home.

1

Arrive

Leave the highway behind. As you pull into the park-like grounds along the river, the pace shifts. Trees, water, and quiet welcome you before the door even opens.

2

Gather

Come together in the River View Great Room or the Gathering Room — spaces designed not just for meetings, but for the kind of connection that happens when people feel held.

3

Create & Learn

Workshops, hands-on crafts, storytelling, music. The Music Room and open grounds invite expression, discovery, and the satisfaction of making something together.

4

Explore

Canoe, hike, bike, cast a line, or simply walk the Kankakee River trail. The natural world here is a living classroom — and a place to simply breathe.

5

Share a Meal

Gather around the table for food curated to your group — gourmet, ethnic, vegan, gluten free. Or fire up the grill as the fire table glows in the evening air.

6

Leave Renewed

What you carry out is different from what you brought in. A clearer mind. New friendships. A deeper sense of what's possible when people gather with intention.
